Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

Real Direct Focus Consultancy ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit realdirectfocus.com (the "Site") or engage our consulting services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us — name, email address, and message content submitted through our contact form or direct email.
  • Engage our services — business information necessary to perform consulting work, as agreed upon in a service engagement.

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. The Site does not use analytics tools that collect personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate about potential engagements.
  • Deliver consulting services as agreed upon.
  • Maintain records of our business communications.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process.
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety.
  • You have given explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., LinkedIn, project sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

7. Client Confidentiality

All client business information shared during consulting engagements is treated as confidential unless otherwise agreed in writing. We do not disclose client names, business details, or project specifics without explicit permission.

8. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Delete your information from our records.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at owner@realdirectfocus.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
owner@realdirectfocus.com
